PCB Preflight API Extract Example

Easily extract the detailed PCB design information required for Manufacturng and Assembly - such as Minimum space/trace and annular rings, Min. Smt Pitch and Pad width, and more. Displays the resulting PCB design information in a formatted table.
  1. Enter your own PCB Preflight API-key in the form below.
  2. Upload your PCB Design manufacturing files (i.e Gerber, NC Drill/Rout, ODB++, IPC-2581, FAB3000 Workspace).
    Note: You can upload a ZIP file or multiple files.
